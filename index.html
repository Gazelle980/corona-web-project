About

The focus of Corona Secondary School Agbara is the development of a world-class school it is as of today. World class in all facets of our operations - curriculum, teaching methodologies, staff quality, libraries, technology, buildings, resources, facilities, management and so on

We take great pride in the warm personal relationships which exist and the involvement of all pupils in some form of community.
